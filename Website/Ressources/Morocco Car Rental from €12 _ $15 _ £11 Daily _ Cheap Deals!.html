Enjoy North Africa’s Finest with Car Hire Morocco

Located in North Africa, Morocco has some of the most exciting places for tourists to visit. This is an Arabic speaking country with a great history, natural features and cultural centers to savor. Whether you are planning for a great family vacation, business tour or private visit, this is a number one spot. History students throng the great country to learn about the amazing Mediterranean, Roman, Phoenician and Islamic history. Archeologists and geologists also find this region resourceful in many ways. You can find accurate information about ancient dynasties, world empires and wars including the struggle by the French and Spanish in the region.

Great Mediterranean Climate

Mountain lovers have a lot to enjoy in the Atlas Mountains. Thanks to car hire in Morocco, visitors can access all corners of the mountains under a rich humid climate. There are numerous forests with unique species of cedars, oaks, junipers among others. This Mediterranean region has visitors all year round.

The country is host to part of the great Sahara desert. This region has unique sceneries of the world desert landscapes. Enjoy taking unique photos of beautiful, rich oases, rocks and sand dunes. Occasionally, you will capture the breadth taking sand storms.

Amazing Wildlife

With such a wonderful climate, you will find exceptional wildlife featuring both animals and plant life. Each region offers a wide variety of wildlife for visitors to sample including the Moroccan lions, bears and leopards. The country is home to some of the world’s endangered species with birds, butterflies and mammals among the lot.

The People

The kind of hospitality you find in Morocco is exemplary. While most of the inhabitants in the country are Arabs, this is a cosmopolitan state with all kinds of religions and cultures. There are Africans, Europeans, Asians and Arabs in the area. Although the official language is Arab, you will find French speaking, Spaniards, Africans and other languages all over the region. The country has people from three dialects of its Berber language which is quite easy to learn.

Business Spot

Morocco is a great travel destination for business travelers looking for investment spots. It has a diverse economic growth scale across different industries. Tourism, telecoms, IT, agriculture and trade feature among the top industries. Shopping lovers will find the best places to buy special gifts for friends and family within the country’s finest malls. While at it, travelers will sample the amazing cuisine in Morocco’s finest hotels and restaurants. There is so much to enjoy from a combination of Moroccan, African, Arabic, Jewish and Anglo American cultures.
